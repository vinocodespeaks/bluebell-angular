All the hotels on our tours are strategically selected for their close proximity to the destinations on our itineraries. Comfort is essential, which is why all of our hotels are three- and four-star rated.

CUSTOMISED ITINERARIES

Our customised itineraries enable students to undergo educational tours to those MNCs according to their interested industries, area of specialization and future career aspirations.

GLOBAL CUISINE

Eating the local cuisine is one of the best and most enjoyable ways to experience a new culture. With BlueBell, there are endless opportunities.Be it mouth watering Italian dishes in New York or Half smokes in Washington DC.

COMFORTABLE TRANSPORTATION

International travel can get a bit tiring . But it doesn't have to be uncomfortable. We make the hassles of transportation easier with flights as direct as possible, 4-berth couchettes on overnight trains and buses that are clean, operated by reputable companies and drivers, and not packed to capacity. So you arrive in a new city feeling refreshed and ready to explore.
